Who am I?

Inotila Opoeli Nghaamwa (B.1991) is an artist and designer born in a small village called Oipapakane in Northern Namibia. A nomad since birth Inotila is currently located in Malmö, Sweden. He has had exhibited in Namibia(The dream,2023) and in Sweden (Odyssey, 2023 ), furthermore his work has been shown around Malmö, having an eight week long exhibition at popular seaside cafe St Jakob’s and showing of similar duration at Thap Thim restaurant located in the luxurious neighbourhood of Västra hamnen. Namibian publication featured Inotila as “Artist in the spot light” 2023, which is a slot reserved for artist whose work has been considered note worthy. However, being the last of 15 children Inotila mastered the art of finding spaces to be alone and is not one to enjoy the spot light and would prefer his work spoke for him. This need to find a space to be alone is reflected in his art which tends to be have isolated figures or objects. Being an avid traveller a strong appreciation for landscapes was developed and his work captures the temperament felt when looking at these landscapes. Using mostly acrylic paint, ink, and pencil Inotila creates images with allegorical meanings for those that dare go that deep, to him a canvas is a portal to new dimension.
