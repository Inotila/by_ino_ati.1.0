Who am I?

Inotila Opoeli Nghaamwa (B.1991) is an artist and designer born in a small village called Oipapakane in Northern Namibia. Having lived between Namibia and South Africa Inotila has always been some what of a nomad and is currently located in Malm√∂, Sweden. The last of 15 children Inotila mastered the art of finding spaces to be alone. This need to find a space to be alone is reflected in his art. Being an avid traveller a strong appreciation for landscapes was developed and his work captures the temperament felt when looking at these landscapes. Using mostly acrylic paint, ink, and pencil Inotila creates images with allegorical meanings for those that dare go that deep or just for those who want to go to a different dimension.
